# Parallel Kingdom Controls - Updated

## How to Play

### First Flag Placement
1. **Right-click** on the map to place your first flag at that location
2. This establishes your starting territory (25m radius)
3. Entities (monsters, resources) spawn within 1km

### Movement
1. **Double-click** on any location on the map
2. Your character auto-walks to that location
3. GPS distance is calculated and energy is consumed
4. Monsters/resources are detected automatically as you move

### Territory Expansion  
1. **Right-click** on a location adjacent to your territory (within 30m)
2. New flag places if the area is unclaimed
3. Each flag claims a 25-meter radius territory
4. You can only expand adjacent to existing territory (Parallel Kingdom rule)

### Combat
- Walk within 10 meters of a monster to trigger combat
- Monster appears in Combat panel on right side
- Click Attack to fight
- Defeat monsters for experience and loot

### Resources
- Walk within 10 meters of a resource node to harvest
- Resources appear on map as emoji markers
- Harvested resources go to your city
- Resources regenerate over time
