# Parallel Kingdom Link Summaries

## Grokipedia – Parallel Kingdom
https://grokipedia.com/page/parallel_kingdom

- GPS/MMORPG overlay on real maps; location‑based exploration and territory control.
- Freemium model, iOS/Android launch dates and growth milestones.
- Core gameplay: claim land with flags/structures, PvE + PvP, dungeons, harvesting.
- Social systems: chat, alliances/kingdoms, trading economy.
- “Ages” updates; later Sky Realm, airships, new metals/amulets, skills.
- Charitable in‑game campaigns; decline and shutdown in 2016.

## Wayback Beginner’s Guide – Page 1 (Overview)
https://web.archive.org/web/20121122202733/http://parallelkingdom.com/guide/en/page1.aspx

- Customization: hats/masks/weapons/armor; gear upgrades and naming items.
- Skill specialization (90+ levels) and custom class building.
- Core loop: claim land, build flags/houses, explore using GPS.
- PvP/PvE: Proving Grounds, Kingdom Wars, Dark Monolith, dungeons.
- Resources listed: wood, leather, oil, crystal, stone.

## Wayback Beginner’s Guide – Page 2 (Stake Your Claim)
https://web.archive.org/web/20121129014653/http://parallelkingdom.com/guide/en/page2.aspx

- Dog Whistle → summon dog → find nearest unclaimed land → build first flag/house.
- Hunting & gathering to repair gear/expand territory.
- Monster tiers: Timid, Normal, Strong, Vicious, Epic.
- Abilities: Taunt, Mourning Berries (heal), Shield Charge.
- Travel: walking dog, relocating, chaining flags as “roads.”

## Wayback Beginner’s Guide – Page 3 (Getting Social)
https://web.archive.org/web/20121129012346/http://parallelkingdom.com/guide/en/page3.aspx

- Chat types: Help, Global, Map; private messaging.
- Friends/Enemies systems; invites and traveling together.
- Cities (black flags), resources unique to cities (Crystal/Oil), mayoral system.
- Kingdoms: kingdom chat, buildings, bricks, permissions/taxation.

## DroidForums Tutorial Thread
https://www.droidforums.net/threads/parallel-kingdom-tutorial-pk.79603/

- Early‑game tips: city ticket → oil well → golem → collect crude oil daily.
- Berries farming and tending trees; backpacks for storage.
- Trade posts add 10% tax; drop‑trade avoids tax but has scam risk.
- Travel: guard towers, roc shrines, roc feathers; purses protect gold on death.
- Weekly hats and activities tied to hat races.
